Government Health & Insurance Plans

SNAP (Food Assistance)

Start Date: 2023-01-01

End Date: 2090-01-01

Provides monthly benefits to low-income families to purchase nutritious food.

Eligibility:

  • Household income below ₹100,000/year.
  • Indian citizens or legal immigrants.
  • Assets below state limits.
CCAP (Child Care Assistance)

Start Date: 2023-06-01

End Date: 2090-01-01

Helps low-income families afford safe, quality childcare while parents work or attend school.

Eligibility:

  • At least one child under 16 years old.
  • Family income below ₹100,000/year.
  • Parents must be working, job hunting, or studying.
MEDAID (Medical Aid)

Start Date: 2023-03-01

End Date: 2090-01-01

Medical insurance for low-income individuals without property income, covering essential health services.

Eligibility:

  • Annual income less than ₹100,000.
  • No owned property generating income.
  • Must meet residency and citizenship requirements.
MEDCARE (Senior Health Plan)

Start Date: 2023-04-01

End Date: 2090-01-01

Health coverage for citizens aged 65+, including hospital stays, outpatient care, and medications.

Eligibility:

  • Must be 65 years of age or older.
  • India. citizen or legal resident for 5+ years.
  • May include automatic enrollment based on Social Security.
CAJW (Youth Assistance)

Start Date: 2023-07-01

End Date: 2090-01-01

Support program for unemployed youth, offering financial aid, skill training, and job placement services.

Eligibility:

  • No employment income.
  • Recently completed education (graduated).
  • Age between 18 and 29 years preferred.
QHP (Qualified Health Plan)

Start Date: 2023-05-01

End Date: 2090-01-01

Comprehensive health insurance for higher-income citizens, covering preventive, emergency, and specialist services.

Eligibility:

  • Annual income greater than ₹100,000.
  • Must not qualify for Medicaid or Medicare.
  • Resident of the India. with valid proof of income.
